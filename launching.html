Stone-Paper-Scissor

Instructions:

  • Choose Your Move:
    Click on one of the images (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) to make your choice.
  • Computer Will Choose:
    After you make your choice, the computer will randomly pick one of the three options (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) as its move.
  • Game Rules:
    • Rock beats Scissors.
    • Scissors beats Paper.
    • Paper beats Rock.
    • If both you and the computer pick the same move, it's a draw.
  • Score System:
    • Every time you win, your score increases by 1.
    • If the computer wins, its score increases by 1.
    • The score is displayed on the screen for both you and the computer.
